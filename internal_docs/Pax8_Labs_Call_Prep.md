# Pax8 Labs Call Prep

## 30-Second Background

"I've spent most of my career building enterprise software platforms and full-stack applications, primarily in TypeScript ecosystems. Recently I've been focused on AI agents, orchestration patterns, Azure AI Foundry, human-in-the-loop workflows, and exploring how AI changes the way knowledge work gets done. That's what caught my attention about the Pax8 Labs role."

---

## What I Know About Pax8

- Pax8 is a large cloud marketplace focused on MSPs.
- MSPs use Pax8 to manage Microsoft, security, backup, identity, and cloud subscriptions.
- Pax8 Labs sounds like a product innovation group rather than the core marketplace business.
- The AI workflow, orchestration, and human-AI collaboration language suggests they may be building new products rather than maintaining existing systems.

---

## Questions to Ask

### What is Pax8 Labs?

> What exactly is Pax8 Labs?

Things to understand:

- Innovation group?
- Incubator?
- Product engineering team?
- Internal AI team?

---

### Who is the Customer?

> Who is the customer for the work Labs is doing?

Possible answers:

- MSP technicians
- MSP business owners
- SMB customers
- Internal Pax8 employees

This may be the most important question.

---

### AI Strategy

> When you say AI workflows and agent orchestration, what kinds of problems are you solving?

Listen for:

- Agents coordinating work
- Copilot experiences
- Workflow automation
- Knowledge systems
- Internal productivity tools

---

### Greenfield vs Existing Products

> How much of the work is greenfield product development versus extending existing platforms?

The more greenfield work, the more likely the role aligns with innovation and platform creation.

---

### Technology Stack

> What's the current stack?

Listen for:

- TypeScript
- React
- Next.js
- Azure
- AWS
- Azure AI Foundry
- OpenAI
- MCP
- Agent frameworks

---

### Success Expectations

> What would someone in this role be expected to accomplish in the first 6–12 months?

This often reveals how mature the vision and roadmap are.

---

### Team Direction

> Where do you see the Labs organization in the next couple years?

Understand whether Labs is a strategic long-term investment or an experimental initiative.

---

## Compensation

If asked:

> What's your target compensation?

Suggested response:

"I'd like to learn more about the role and scope before anchoring too hard. Given my background in full-stack platform engineering and AI-related work, I'd expect something competitive with senior or staff-level engineering roles in the Denver market, but I'm open to discussing the full package."

Approximate ranges:

- Senior Engineer: $170k–210k total compensation
- Staff Engineer: $200k–260k+ total compensation

Avoid anchoring too low early in the process.

---

## The One Question That Matters Most

If there is time for only one question:

> What problem is Pax8 Labs trying to solve that Pax8 couldn't solve with a traditional product engineering team?

The answer will reveal whether Labs is:

- A serious innovation group
- An AI-first product initiative
- A traditional engineering team with a new label
