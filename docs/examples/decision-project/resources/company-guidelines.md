# Company Decision-Making Guidelines

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Growth** - Prioritize sustainable revenue growth
2. **Profitability** - Maintain healthy margins (>40%)
3. **Risk Management** - Avoid downside risks > 20%
4. **Market Position** - Strengthen competitive advantage

## Decision Criteria

### Financial Criteria

- **Minimum ROI**: 20% for new initiatives
- **Payback Period**: < 3 years preferred
- **NPV Threshold**: Positive NPV required
- **Risk Adjusted Return**: Account for uncertainty

### Strategic Criteria

- **Alignment**: Must align with 3-year strategy
- **Competitive Impact**: Strengthen market position or maintain
- **Talent**: Consider impact on key talent
- **Customer Impact**: Positive or neutral impact required

### Risk Criteria

- **Execution Risk**: Assess feasibility
- **Market Risk**: Assess demand uncertainty
- **Regulatory Risk**: Compliance and regulatory impact
- **Reputational Risk**: Brand and reputation impact

## Escalation Matrix

| Decision Size | Approval Required | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| < $500K | Department Head | 5 business days |
| $500K - $2M | VP | 10 business days |
| $2M - $10M | CFO + CEO | 15 business days |
| > $10M | Board | 30 business days |

## Analysis Requirements

For decisions > $500K:

1. Written analysis required
2. Financial model with sensitivity analysis
3. Risk assessment with mitigation plans
4. Stakeholder impact assessment
5. 3 options evaluated minimum

## Document Template

Include in all analyses:

- Executive Summary (1 page)
- Decision Context and Background
- Options with pros/cons
- Financial Analysis
- Strategic Assessment
- Risk Analysis
- Recommendation with Rationale
- Next Steps and Implementation
